üéØ Competition Rounds Explained

Sprint Round

  • 30 questions
  • 40 minutes
  • Individual work
  • NO calculator
  • Tests speed & accuracy

Target Round

  • 8 questions (4 pairs)
  • 6 minutes per pair
  • Individual work
  • Calculator allowed
  • Tests problem-solving

Team Round

  • 10 questions
  • 20 minutes
  • Groups of 4
  • Calculator allowed
  • UNOFFICIAL - For Fun!

Countdown Round

  • Top 12 students only
  • Head-to-head matches
  • 45 seconds per problem
  • NO calculator
  • Determines School Team
Note about Team Round: The Team Round is unofficial and for practice only. It does not count toward individual rankings for Chapter Competition selection. Students will work collaboratively in groups of 4 at their tables.
