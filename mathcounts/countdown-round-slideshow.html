Countdown Round Tournament Format

Sequential Ladder

  • 10 Students compete in sequential ladder matches
  • Match 1: #10 faces #9
  • Match 2: Winner stays to face #8
  • Match 3: Winner stays to face #7
  • Matches 4-9: Winner continues up the ladder facing #6, #5, #4, #3, #2, #1
  • Winner stays, loser is eliminated
  • The final winner is the Countdown Round champion!

Countdown Round Instructions

Part 1: Problem Presentation & Buzzer Rules

  • I will read each problem aloud as it is presented to you
  • You may use the scratch paper and pencil in front of you to calculate your answer
  • Calculators are NOT allowed during this round
  • You will have a maximum of 60 seconds to solve the problem after it is presented
  • You will be given a ten-second warning before time expires
  • As soon as you have solved the problem, press your buzzer
  • I will call on the first person who signals
  • Do not announce your answer until I call on you

Countdown Round Instructions

Part 2: Answering & Scoring

  • Each time you wish to answer, you must signal with your buzzer
  • You may not answer more than once for any question
  • If you do not signal before you answer, your answer will be disqualified
  • If you answer after signaling but before I call on you, your answer will be accepted, but please wait until you hear your name
  • Once I call on you, you will have three seconds to begin your answer
  • Your opponent may continue working while you are responding
  • If you answer correctly, you will score one point in the round
  • If you answer incorrectly, your opponent will have the remainder of the allotted 60 seconds to buzz for an opportunity to answer

Countdown Round Instructions

Part 3: Winning the Match

  • Whoever answers the most of the three problems correctly (not necessarily two out of three) will progress to the next round
  • If you are tied after three questions, I will declare a sudden victory situation
  • I will describe the rules for this process should this situation arise
  • IMPORTANT: If you answer without signaling your buzzer, your answer will be disqualified
