About Our Math Club

The Hallie Wells Middle School Competition Math Club is dedicated to fostering a love of mathematics through challenging and engaging competitions. Our club provides students with opportunities to develop problem-solving skills, mathematical thinking, and teamwork while having fun with math.

We welcome students of all skill levels from grades 6-8. Whether you're just beginning your mathematical journey or you're already passionate about problem-solving, our club has something for everyone. Our experienced coach, Prof. Jojo, guides students through various competition formats and helps them build confidence in their mathematical abilities.

Competitions We Participate In:

  • AMC 8: American Mathematics Competitions for grades 8 and below
  • MATHCOUNTS: National middle school mathematics competition
  • MOEMS: Mathematical Olympiads for Elementary and Middle Schools
  • Purple Comet! Math Meet: Online team-based mathematics competition
  • Math Kangaroo: International mathematics competition for students
  • Noetic Learning Math Contest: Semiannual problem-solving competition

Our regular practice sessions focus on developing problem-solving strategies, learning mathematical concepts beyond the standard curriculum, and building the confidence needed to excel in competitive mathematics. We also emphasize collaboration and peer learning, creating a supportive environment where students can learn from each other.
