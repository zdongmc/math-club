Noetic Learning & Purple Comet Sign-Ups Now Open!
February 11, 2026
Two new competition sign-ups are now available in the Parent Portal!

Noetic Learning Math Contest
The Noetic Learning Math Contest is a national math competition for students in grades 2-8, with over 60,000 participants nationwide. Students solve grade-level problems that emphasize critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Top performers earn medals (team winners and top 10% nationally) and ribbons (top 50%).

• Date: Friday, April 10, 2026, 3:15 - 4:15 PM
• Deadline to sign up: March 1, 2026
• Cost: $99 per team of up to 30 students, split among all team members. Fees will be collected via SchoolCash Online the first week of March.
• 6th and 7th graders can choose to compete at their own grade level or on the 8th/Mixed team

Purple Comet Math Meet
The Purple Comet Math Meet is a free online team math competition for middle and high school students, held annually since 2003. Teams from around the world work together to solve challenging math problems.

• Date: Friday, April 17, 2026, 3:15 - 4:30 PM
• Deadline to form teams: April 1, 2026
• Free online team competition — teams of 1-6 students
• Create a team, join an open team, or get invited to a team

Don't miss these opportunities! Sign up early to secure your spot.
