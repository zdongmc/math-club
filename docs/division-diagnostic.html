What are these diagnostics for?

The Montgomery Blair Math Tournament (MBMT) has two divisions:

  • Abel Division (Lower) - Generally recommended for newer competitors
  • Jacobi Division (Upper) - Generally recommended for experienced competitors

These diagnostic tools help you:

  • Determine which division is right for you
  • Identify which 2 subjects you should choose for the individual round
  • Build confidence by practicing with real MBMT problems

How to Use These Diagnostics

  • Complete all four diagnostics to receive personalized recommendations
  • Be honest with yourself when rating difficulty - this helps you find the right level
  • Work at your own pace - you can complete the diagnostics in any order
  • Discuss with Prof. Jojo if you have questions about which division or subjects to choose
  • Remember: The goal is to find the right challenge level where you can perform your best and enjoy the competition!
